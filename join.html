Crew Prerequisites

  • Must be at-least Level 15+
  • Must be at-least Age 15+
  • Must not have a extensive punishment history
  • Must have a working microphone (and be willing to use it)
  • Must speak fluent English (does not need to be primary language)

Attention

Please note their are no exceptions to the requirements, it is mandatory that an applicant meets all of them. If you have any questions or concerns you may contact a Captain via his or her Direct Messages
